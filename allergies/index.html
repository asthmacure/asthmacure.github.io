Allergies

The following are recommended for coping with any allergies or difficulties during seasonal changes:

  • Raw milk - Natural enzymes, fat, vitamins and minerals with healing bacteria. Pasteurised milk is not recommended and homogenised milk is not fit for human consumption.
  • Raw unheated honey - A product of nature with many documented health benefits, it will accustom the body to pollen collected to make the honey. If the honey is heated after extraction it isn't going to be useful.
  • Ascorbic acid (vitamin C) - A deficiency may play a role in allergies. Supplementing is not recommended, but getting it from a natural source, such as unpasteurised orange juice.

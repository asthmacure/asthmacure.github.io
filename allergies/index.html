Allergies

I recommend the following for coping with any allergies and difficulties during seasonal changes:

  • Raw milk - Natural enzymes, fat, vitamins and minerals with healing bacteria. I do not recommend pasteurised milk and homogenised milk is not fit for human consumption.
  • Raw unheated honey - A natural process of nature with many documented health benefits, it will accustom you to pollen collected to make the honey. If the honey is heated after extraction it isn't going to be useful.
  • Ascorbic acid (vitamin C) - A deficiency may play a role in allergies. I wouldn't recommend supplementing, but getting it from a natural source, such as unpasteurised orange juice.

Introduction

Along with asthma I have cured myself of other related health problems, such as hay fever, allergies, eczema and dermatitis. I'm not a doctor, a nutritionist or a psychologist and I don't seek money for this. I don't claim to have all the answers, but I believe I have a moral obligation to publish my information and offer implementable advice.

Searching for any of the conditions above will return information about how to manage or control the symptoms. A lot of useful information has been taken out of circulation, is difficult to obtain or exists behind paywalls.
